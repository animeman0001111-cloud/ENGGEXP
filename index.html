PROBLEM STATEMENT

Design a ladybug-inspired model as an Insectronics system to achieve safe indoor navigation and basic environmental awareness with clear visual indication of object detection and battery status using LEDs.The model is limited to a compact size of 18–20 cm, a total weight below 2 kg, an operating voltage range of 5–12 V, and a total cost not exceeding ₹5,000, ensuring energy efficiency, affordability, and suitability for indoor use. The system moves forward, senses obstacles and turns left or right to avoid collisions, monitors temperature and air quality, and provides system status indication through LEDsrotates its head or sensor unit to scan surroundings, measures the distance to nearby objects, and displays battery level information using an LED indicator.🐞🐞

MOTOR SELECTION CALCULATIONS

⚙️ Force & Torque Calculation

Step 1 — Weight Force
F = m × g
F = 0.7 × 9.81
F = 6.867 N
Step 2 — Torque (T = F × r)
r = wheel radius = 0.0636 m
T = 6.867 × 0.0636
T = 0.4367 N·m
Step 3 — Convert to kg·cm
T = 0.4367 × 10.197
T = 4.45 kg·cm

🛡️ Safety Factor & Final Selection

Mass of Robot (m) 0.7 kg
Gravity (g) 9.81 m/s²

Safety Factor = 1.8
