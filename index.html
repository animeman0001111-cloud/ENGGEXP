PROBLEM STATEMENT

Indoor environments such as homes,classrooms, and offices often lack simple systemsthat can continuously monitor surrounding conditions and detect obstacleswithout human supervision. This creates a need for a small, intelligent device that can move around, observe environmental conditions, and respond to nearby objects to avoid collisions. A "ladybug-inspired Insectronics model" is proposed as a compact mobile system capable of sensing temperature, air quality, and obstacles in its path while navigating safely by turning when an object is detected in front of it. The system is intended to provide timely alerts about environmental changes while remaining energy-efficient, affordable, and small enough to be easily deployed in indoorspaces. 🐞
