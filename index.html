CONVERSATION BETWEEN CLEINT AND DESIGNER

No Questions Answers Objective Function Constraint
1 What problem are we solving with Insectornic? It should help reduce crop damage by monitoring harmful insects in farms. ✔️
2 Who is the target user? Small and medium-scale farmers mostly. ✔️
3 Should it detect all insects? No, focus only on harmful insects like locusts and beetles. ✔️
4 Should it only detect insects or also control them? It should detect insects first and then activate a safe control method. ✔️
5 What type of power source is preferred? Solar power, since farms may not have reliable electricity. ✔️
6 Do you require real-time alerts? Yes, alerts should be sent through SMS or a mobile app. ✔️
7 Where will the system be installed? It will be used outdoors in farm fields. ✔️
8 What is the expected budget range? It should be affordable for farmers. ✔️
9 What lifespan do you expect? At least 3 to 5 years of reliable operation. ✔️
10 Is manual control necessary? Yes, farmers should have a manual override option. ✔️
11 What type of alert system is required? Both sound alarms and mobile notifications. ✔️
12 How often should maintenance be required? Preferably minimal, maybe once every six months. ✔️
13 Do you want GPS tracking? Basic location tracking would be beneficial. ✔️
14 Are future upgrades expected? Yes, future upgrades can improve insect detection accuracy and add smart features. ✔️
15 Why is sensor rotation useful? To allow the robot to scan a wider area for objects. ✔️
16 Why should the robot provide visual indicators (LED)? To quickly inform users about object detection and battery status. ✔️
17 Why should the system be affordable? To make it accessible for general users and small applications. ✔️
18 Why is obstacle detection important in the system? To help the robot avoid collisions and move safely. ✔️
19 Why should the robot move autonomously? To monitor surroundings continuously without manual control. ✔️
20 Why is energy efficiency important for the system? To allow longer operation with limited battery power. ✔️
